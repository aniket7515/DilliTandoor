Murg Dhaniya Adraki

Murg Dhaniya Adraki is a vibrant and aromatic Indian dish that celebrates the rich flavors of coriander (dhaniya) and ginger (adraki). Tender pieces of chicken are marinated and simmered in a hearty sauce, bursting with the freshness of green coriander and the warm, piquant notes of ginger.
